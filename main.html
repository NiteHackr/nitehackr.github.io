April 18, 2024 @4:31pm

    I haven't posted on here for a very long time now, but I wanted to let people know what has happened to me.  In March of 2021 my wife Wanda (pictured above), the one whome I made "Deluxe Pacman" for, suddenly had a ministroke.  It only lasted a minute or so, but subsequent scans revealed that she had cancer.  Within six months of that initial stroke, she breathed her last and died on September 21, 2021.  I have been devestated as we knew each other since my teens and married when I turned 20 in 1985.  She was the world to me, we were married almost 37 years.  I cried daily for the first year and a half and on and off since then.  Losing your spouse after together so long, saying "I love you" daily is worse than anyone could imagine.  Anyhow, not to depress anyone, but I wanted to let you all know what has happened.  If it wasn't for Wanda, I would never have made the game.  Her picture is in the credits of Deluxe Pacman 2, beside her best friend, who I still talk to.  I have thought about recreating a third version of the game, a "memorial" edition in memory of her.  I'm still uncertain as my enthusasiam and general motivations in life seems to have left me; time will tell.  Anyhow, love you all, thanks to the few that donated.  Trust me, I could never have lived off the donations for this!  Hehehe... not that I done it for the money, I did not.  I may have gotten $10 a year on average, if that.  But no, I done it for her and can't wait until I see her again in Christ's kingdom, God willing.

    If you have a spouse, make certain you tell them you love them daily as you never know when they will be taken away someday.  My final words to Wanda was "I love you" and hers to me was "I love you too", two days later she stopped breathing.
